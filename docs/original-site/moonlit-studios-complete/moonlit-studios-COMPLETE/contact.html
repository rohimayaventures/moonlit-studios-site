Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical response time?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours, usually much faster. For urgent projects, mention "ASAP" in your message.

Do you offer rush services?

Yes! Rush projects are available at 2x standard pricing. We can often deliver landing pages in 3-5 days and full websites in 2-3 weeks.

What's included in a discovery call?

30-minute video call to discuss your project goals, timeline, budget, and technical requirements. We'll provide a custom quote and project plan.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely! Many of our clients are startups and entrepreneurs. We offer flexible payment plans and can scale services to your budget.

What if I'm not sure what I need?

No problem! That's what discovery calls are for. We'll help you define your requirements and recommend the best approach.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes! We offer maintenance packages, monthly retainers, and per-hour support after project completion.
